Why NORA?

One Icon. Two Ways to Enjoy.

Nora Classic Lemon Iced Tea is crafted to play two roles — your everyday refresher and your party's favourite mixer. Real brewed tea, real lemon, a touch of honey. Chill it, pour it, or mix it. Your bottle, your rules.

The Refresher
Your Everyday Refresher.

Real Indian tea leaves, slow-brewed and kissed with natural lemon. Crisp, clean, and gently sweetened with honey — the perfect answer to Indian summers, long commutes, post-workout thirst, and 4 PM tea-break boredom.

  • Real brewed tea — never from powder
  • Natural Indian lemon flavour
  • A touch of honey — no refined sugar
  • No artificial colours
  • Vegetarian-friendly

Best enjoyed: chilled at 4°C, over ice with a sprig of mint — with lunch, after cricket, and everywhere in between.

Who We Are

Born in Bharat. Brewed for India.

Nora was born from a simple belief: India doesn't need to import its refreshment. Our soil grows the world's finest tea. Our orchards give us the juiciest lemons. Our traditions taught us that a cool drink on a hot afternoon is not a luxury — it's a ritual.

From the tea gardens of Assam and the Nilgiris to the lemon groves of Maharashtra and Andhra, every Nora bottle carries the flavour of Indian earth and the spirit of Indian craft. We brew real tea leaves — no powders, no concentrates — and balance them with natural lemon, so every sip tastes like it was made at home. Because it was. Made in India, by Indian hands, from Indian ingredients.

🇮🇳 100% Made in India 🌿 Real Brewed Tea Leaves 🍋 Natural Indian Lemon 🍯 Lemon & Honey, No Refined Sugar 🤝 Sourced from Indian Farms

Our Vision

To make Bharat's own iced tea the first choice of a new, health-conscious India — a homegrown brand that stands proudly on every shelf beside global giants, and proves to the world that honest, world-class refreshment can be brewed on Indian soil, from Indian ingredients, for Indian tastes.

Our Mission

To craft honest refreshment from Indian tea, lemon and honey — with no shortcuts and nothing artificial — while supporting Indian farmers through fair, direct sourcing, creating livelihoods in the regions we source from, and bringing people together over a cold glass of something real: one bottle, one gathering, one Indian summer at a time.
